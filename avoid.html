Tools and Mods to Avoid

This page is not a permanent ban list. It is a mobile-first caution list for this guide.

Tools to avoid for this guide

Tool/categoryStatusReason
ENBAvoidNot a good fit for this Android/GameNative target.
WabbajackAvoidDesktop MO2 automation does not match the current DML mobile setup.
Automated LOD generation on AndroidAvoidUseful later, but too many generated files and too many variables for this guide right now.
Old New Vegas mod managersAvoidThis guide is written for DML. Old managers make troubleshooting harder.
Random INI packsAvoidUse BethINI Pie and your own saved INIs instead.

Mod categories to avoid for now

CategoryStatusReason
Generated LOD setupsAvoidNeeds its own Android path later.
Huge texture packsTestingCan work, but storage and memory cost climb fast. Test separately.
Heavy grass or landscape overhaulsTestingExtra render cost and more variables.
Heavy particle overhaulsAvoidTest separately with FPS and crash logs.
Picture-in-picture scope setupsTestingCan be expensive on mobile. Test after the main guide.
Large NPC face/body overhaulsTestingAsset-heavy and patch-heavy.
Big quest/worldspace stacksTestingAdd one at a time after the guide list works.

Safe modding rule

  • Do not keep adding mods after a failed launch.
  • Do not change Proton, Turnip, DXVK, BethINI, and mods all in one test.
  • Do not assume desktop advice applies cleanly to Android.
  • When benchmarking, change one thing and write down what changed.
